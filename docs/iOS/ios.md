# iOS Setup

## Supervize the device
Use Apple Configurator to configure the device and Supervize it.
Setup the device as minimal as possible, just to get to home screen.

## MDM Enrollment
Login to JAMF Now and enable Open Enrollment Profile. 
Scan QR code and enroll the device.

Apps and configuration will be automatically installed on the device.
Make sure the device stays connected to wifi.
